Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna recipe will have your mouth watering! Lasagna is one of my favorite foods. It is very easy to make and very filling. I hope you enjoy this homestyle recipe.

Ingredients

  • Hamburger - 1lb 80/20 hamburger is fine.
  • Oven ready lasagna noodles - Use oven baked for covenience.
  • Cottage cheese - Any brand will work. We use whole milk large curd.
  • Spices - Powdered garlic, pepper, and onion powder is all you need.
  • Spaghetti sauce - Use your preferred sauce. We really like Rao's or Aldis brand.
  • Parmesean cheese - Use any brand you would like.
  • Mozzerella cheese - We like sargento but store brand works just fine.

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large pan add spices and cook 1lb of hamburger over medium heat. Drain grease.
  3. After your hamburger is cooked. Add spaghetti sauce and cottage cheese. Stir occasionally.
  4. While the lasagna sauce is warming up. Grease baking pan.
  5. Put one layer of lasgana noodles on the bottom of baking pan.
  6. Your lasagna sauce should be ready. Add layer of sauce on top of noodles.
  7. Alternate noodles and sauce until you are out of both.
  8. Make sure the noodles on top have sauce on them do not leave them bare.
  9. Fill up spaghetti jar 1/4-1/2 of water and add to baking pan. The noodles will be dry if you don't add water.
  10. Cover with tin foil and bake for 35 minutes.
  11. Once lasagna has been baked for 35 minutes uncover and add 2 cups of mozzerella cheese.
  12. Add parmesean cheese to your preference. Then baked uncovered for additional 5 minutes.
  13. Once finished let lasagna sit for 5-10 minutes. Cut then serve.
